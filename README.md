# Tech-Company-Employee-Analytics-dashboard

##  Quick Summary
This repository contains an interactive HR Intelligence Dashboard that monitors corporate workforce dynamics. The report translates raw employee data into strategic insights regarding company demographics, compensation structures, and operational flexibility across North America.

##  Executive Metrics & Insights

###  Workforce Composition
* **Headcount & Tenure:** Active management of **1,000 employees** with an experienced baseline averaging **8.09 years of service** and an average age of **39.97**.
* **Department Split:** Talent is evenly distributed across core business units, led by **Management (186)** and **Product (174)**.
* **Gender Representation:** Tracks male-to-female ratios across teams. The **Product** department leads in female representation (**53.45%**), while **HR** shows the highest male percentage (**57.72%**).

###  Compensation & Performance
* **Financial Baseline:** The organization maintains an **Average Salary of $91.98K USD**.
* **Merit Mapping:** Average performance stands strong at **8.27/10**. The data maps salary distributions directly against performance scores (ranging from 6.5 to 10) to ensure equitable pay-for-performance.

###  Mobility & Geography
* **Hub Locations:** Mapped footprint across major North American tech hubs.
* **Remote Footprint:** **Seattle** and **New York** emerge as the top cities driving the company's remote work volume.

##  Tech Stack & Architecture
* **Platform:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Engine:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for complex HR indexing, gender ratios, and dynamic cross-filtering.
* **UI Design:** Customized Dark Mode theme engineered for high-contrast accessibility and seamless executive reporting.
